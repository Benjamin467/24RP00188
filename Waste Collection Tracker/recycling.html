Recycling Process

How We Process Your Recycling

At our state-of-the-art facility in Gatsibo, we sort and process recyclable materials to minimize waste going to landfills. Our process helps recover valuable resources and reduces environmental impact.

1

Collection

Materials are collected from designated recycling bins throughout Gatsibo. Our fleet of specialized trucks collects recyclables on scheduled days.

2

Sorting

Materials are sorted by type using both automated systems and manual inspection. This ensures proper categorization of different recyclables.

3

Processing

Different materials undergo specific processing to prepare them for reuse. This may involve cleaning, shredding, melting, or chemical treatments.

4

Distribution

Processed materials are sent to manufacturers to create new products. This completes the recycling loop and gives materials a second life.

Plastic Recycling

  • Rinse containers before recycling
  • Remove caps and lids
  • Flatten bottles to save space
  • Check for recycling numbers 1-7
  • Do not include plastic bags or wraps

Paper Recycling

  • Keep paper dry and clean
  • Remove staples and paper clips
  • Flatten cardboard boxes
  • Avoid recycling waxed or laminated paper
  • Remove plastic windows from envelopes

Glass Recycling

  • Rinse containers thoroughly
  • Remove lids and corks
  • Separate by color if required
  • Do not include light bulbs or mirrors
  • Handle broken glass with care
